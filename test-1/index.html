INTERIOR DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

The BMW M interior redefines comfort and sportiness with meticulously designed performance seats. Wrapped in premium Merino leather, the seats offer a perfect balance between luxurious softness and firm support. The cabin's dual-tone design, highlighted by deep blue leather and rich brown accents, creates a bold yet elegant atmosphere. Together with the driver-focused layout, it transforms every journey into an immersive experience.

The BMW Curved Display seamlessly integrates a fully digital instrument cluster with a high-resolution infotainment screen, creating a futuristic driver-focused environment. The M steering wheel, wrapped in premium leather with tri-color stitching, delivers both comfort and control. With ergonomically placed multifunction buttons and red M1/M2 performance toggles, drivers can instantly switch between custom driving modes — from comfort cruising to track-ready dynamics.

The rear seating of this BMW is crafted to deliver unmatched comfort, transforming every drive into a luxurious retreat. Designed with ergonomically contoured seats, passengers enjoy generous legroom, optimal lumbar support, and the finest upholstery materials for lasting relaxation. Premium cushioning adapts to every posture, ensuring long-distance journeys remain fatigue-free.

The rear of this BMW is designed to be a first-class lounge on wheels, offering passengers advanced rear-seat entertainment displays for an immersive travel experience. Mounted seamlessly behind the front seats, these high-resolution screens allow rear occupants to enjoy movies, stream content, or even stay productive on the go.

EXTERIOR DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

The BMW M4's headlights are more than just a source of illumination — they're a signature design statement. Crafted with precision, the slim laser light units feature BMW's Iconic Glow daytime running lights, radiating a distinctive presence both day and night. With BMW Laserlight technology, the high-beam range extends up to 600 meters, ensuring unmatched clarity and confidence at high speeds.

The BMW M4 is equipped with high-performance tyres engineered for maximum grip and agility. Specially developed in collaboration with leading tyre manufacturers, these tyres ensure that every ounce of the M4's 510 horsepower is transferred to the road with confidence. The wider rear tyres deliver outstanding traction during acceleration, while the low-profile front tyres sharpen steering response.

The BMW M4's rear lights are a masterpiece of modern design, combining cutting-edge technology with unmistakable BMW character. Featuring sleek 3D LED taillights, the backlights create a sharp, futuristic appearance that leaves a lasting impression both day and night. The distinctive L-shaped light signature emphasizes the wide stance of the M4.

The BMW M4's fuel refilling system is designed with precision and driver convenience in mind. Featuring a capless refueling design, the system allows you to refuel quickly and cleanly without handling a traditional fuel cap. Simply open the fuel door, insert the nozzle, and you're ready to go — reducing spills and ensuring a secure seal every time.
